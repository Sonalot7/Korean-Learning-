Phrases for Foreigners in Korea

Important Everyday & Emergency Phrases

1. 저 외국인이에요 (jeo oegugin-ieyo) – I'm a foreigner
2. 한국어 잘 못해요 (hangugeo jal mothaeyo) – I can't speak Korean well
3. 영어 할 수 있어요? (yeongeo hal su isseoyo?) – Can you speak English?
4. 다시 말해 주세요 (dasi malhae juseyo) – Please repeat
5. 천천히 말해 주세요 (cheoncheonhi malhae juseyo) – Please speak slowly
6. 도와주세요 (dowajuseyo) – Please help me
7. 길을 잃었어요 (gireul ilheosseoyo) – I’m lost
8. 여기 어디예요? (yeogi eodiyeyo?) – Where am I?
9. 지도 있어요? (jido isseoyo?) – Do you have a map?
10. 이 주소로 가 주세요 (i jusoro ga juseyo) – Please take me to this address
11. 병원 어디예요? (byeongwon eodiyeyo?) – Where is the hospital?
12. 아파요 (apayo) – I'm hurt / sick
13. 응급상황이에요 (eunggeupsanghwang-ieyo) – It's an emergency
14. 경찰 불러 주세요 (gyeongchal bulleo juseyo) – Call the police
15. 도난당했어요 (donandangaesseoyo) – I've been robbed
16. 여권 잃어버렸어요 (yeogwon ilheobeoryeosseoyo) – I lost my passport
17. 길 좀 알려 주세요 (gil jom allyeo juseyo) – Please give me directions
18. 여기까지 얼마예요? (yeogikkaji eolmayeyo?) – How much to get here?
19. 계산서 주세요 (gyesanseo juseyo) – Please give me the bill
20. 카드 돼요? (kadeu dwaeyo?) – Do you take card?
21. 환불 돼요? (hwanbul dwaeyo?) – Can I get a refund?
22. 추천해 주세요 (chucheon hae juseyo) – Please recommend something
23. 이거 뭐예요? (igeo mwoyeyo?) – What is this?
24. 알레르기 있어요 (allereugi isseoyo) – I have an allergy
25. 매운 거 못 먹어요 (maeun geo mot meogeoyo) – I can’t eat spicy food
26. 물 주세요 (mul juseyo) – Water please
27. 화장실 어디예요? (hwajangsil eodiyeyo?) – Where is the bathroom?
28. 약국 있어요? (yakkuk isseoyo?) – Is there a pharmacy nearby?
29. 택시 불러 주세요 (taeksi bulleo juseyo) – Please call a taxi
30. 지하철역 어디예요? (jihacheol-yeok eodiyeyo?) – Where is the subway station?
31. 이거 잃어버렸어요 (igeo ilheobeoryeosseoyo) – I lost this
32. 와이파이 비밀번호 뭐예요? (waipai bimilbeonho mwoyeyo?) – What is the Wi‑Fi password?
33. 사진 찍어 주세요 (sajin jjigeo juseyo) – Please take a photo for me
34. 몇 시에 닫아요? (myeot sie dadayo?) – What time do you close?
35. 길 건너야 해요? (gil geonneoya haeyo?) – Should I cross the street?
36. 위험해요 (wiheomhaeyo) – It’s dangerous
37. 천천히 가 주세요 (cheoncheonhi ga juseyo) – Please drive slowly
38. 여기에 써 주세요 (yeogie sseo juseyo) – Please write it here
39. 통역 필요해요 (tongyeok piryohaeyo) – I need a translator
40. 호텔로 가 주세요 (hotello ga juseyo) – Please take me to my hotel
41. 짐 좀 도와주세요 (jim jom dowajuseyo) – Please help with my luggage
42. 가까운 ATM 있어요? (gakkaun ATM isseoyo?) – Is there an ATM nearby?
43. 환전 어디서 해요? (hwanjeon eodiseo haeyo?) – Where can I exchange money?
44. 한국 처음이에요 (hanguk cheoeum-ieyo) – It’s my first time in Korea
45. 천천히 설명해 주세요 (cheoncheonhi seolmyeonghae juseyo) – Please explain slowly
46. 안전한 길이에요? (anjeonhan gil-ieyo?) – Is it a safe route?
47. 가까운 편의점 어디예요? (gakkaun pyeonuijeom eodiyeyo?) – Where is the nearest convenience store?
48. 제 전화 배터리 없어요 (je jeonhwa baeteori eopseoyo) – My phone battery is dead
49. 충전기 있어요? (chungjeongi isseoyo?) – Do you have a charger?
50. 도와주셔서 감사합니다 (dowajusyeoseo gamsahamnida) – Thank you for helping me
