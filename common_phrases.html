Common Korean Phrases

100 Essential Phrases

1. 안녕하세요 (annyeonghaseyo) – Hello
2. 감사합니다 (gamsahamnida) – Thank you
3. 죄송합니다 (joesonghamnida) – I'm sorry
4. 네 (ne) – Yes
5. 아니요 (aniyo) – No
6. 제발 (jebal) – Please
7. 몰라요 (mollayo) – I don't know
8. 괜찮아요 (gwaenchanayo) – It's okay
9. 어디예요? (eodiyeyo?) – Where is it?
10. 얼마예요? (eolmayeyo?) – How much is it?
11. 잘 지냈어요? (jal jinaesseoyo?) – How have you been?
12. 이름이 뭐예요? (ireumi mwoyeyo?) – What's your name?
13. 만나서 반가워요 (mannaseo bangawoyo) – Nice to meet you
14. 못 알아들었어요 (mot aradeureosseoyo) – I didn't understand
15. 천천히 말해 주세요 (cheoncheonhi malhae juseyo) – Please speak slowly
16. 다시 말해 주세요 (dasi malhae juseyo) – Please repeat
17. 맛있어요 (masisseoyo) – It's delicious
18. 배고파요 (baegopayo) – I'm hungry
19. 피곤해요 (pigonaeyo) – I'm tired
20. 어디 가요? (eodi gayo?) – Where are you going?
21. 좋아요 (joayo) – I like it
22. 싫어요 (sireo) – I don't like it
23. 잠깐만요 (jamkkanmanyo) – Wait a moment
24. 알겠어요 (algesseoyo) – I got it
25. 도와주세요 (dowajuseyo) – Please help me
26. 응 (eung) – Yeah (casual)
27. 아니 (ani) – No (casual)
28. 잠시만요 (jamsimanyo) – Just a moment
29. 이거 뭐예요? (igeo mwoyeyo?) – What is this?
30. 어디 살아요? (eodi sarayo?) – Where do you live?
31. 천만에요 (cheonmaneyo) – You're welcome
32. 축하해요 (chukahaeyo) – Congratulations
33. 잘했어요 (jalhaesseoyo) – Good job
34. 화이팅! (hwaiting!) – Fighting / You can do it!

40. 아파요 (apayo) – It hurts
41. 좋아해요 (joahaeyo) – I like you
42. 이해했어요 (ihaehaesseoyo) – I understand
43. 이해 못했어요 (ihae mothaesseoyo) – I didn't understand
44. 시간 있어요? (sigan isseoyo?) – Do you have time?
45. 뭐 하고 있어요? (mwo hago isseoyo?) – What are you doing?
46. 진짜요? (jinjjayo?) – Really?
47. 거짓말! (geojinmal!) – Lie!
48. 대박! (daebak!) – Awesome!
49. 너무 좋아요 (neomu joayo) – I love it
50. 무슨 뜻이에요? (museun tteusieyo?) – What does it mean?
